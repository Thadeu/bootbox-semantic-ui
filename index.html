Bootbox para Semantic-UI
Para documentação do Bootbox acesse: http://bootboxjs.com/#usage

Exemplo de uso:

Alert Normal

alert("Bootbox SemantUI");

Alert Bootbox Semantic-UI

bootbox.alert("Bootbox SemantUI");

Confirm Bootbox Semantic-UI

bootbox.confirm("Are you sure?", function(result) {
this.show("Confirm result: "+result);
});
